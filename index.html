Invitation

Invite your partner to start this amazing journey that both of you are about to start together. Explain very simply that you would love to deepen your love with your partner by having a beatiful date with him/her. Invite next your partner to spend between 15 to 30 mins to perform the initiation.

Initiation
15 mins - 30 mins

Sit in front of each other, legs crossed. Be sure to explain that you will pick a random date. This date can be playful, sensual, erotic, orgasmic, etc... You don't necessarly know yet. However, the goal is to enjoy the journey and not the destination. There is no obligation to perform any act, this is an invitation to be truly yourself.
Both partners will take turns by asking the following 3 questions:

  1. - What do you truly desire?
  2. - What are you afraid of?
  3. - What do you love about me?
