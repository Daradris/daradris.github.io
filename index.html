Before You Play
Invitation
5 Min

This is your first step toward this amazing journey. Invite your partner(s) to share a moment of playfulness, love & intimacy. We recommend that you sit together. Show your love, passion and longing to to deepen your relationship by having a date night. Be sure that all of you agree on a day and time to start.

Sacred Space
15 Min - 30 Min

If you are playing Random date night for the first time, we recommend that your partner(s) and you set a sacred space in the room. The sacred space is the place where you will always meet up before performing your sacred ritual. In order to perform the initial ritual, you need to set a sacred space with your partner(s). This space can range from a tiny corner of the room where you have set some pillows and candles, or a dedicated playroom. Most importantly, this space should be a place of mutual respect, love, compassion and devotion. So be creative with your partner(s), be sacred!

Sacred Ritual
15 Min - 30 Min

Every date should start with this step, this will become your ritual. The ritual allow partner(s) to meet up with the right mind to ignite their inner fire. Sit in front of each other, legs crossed. Be sure to explain that you will pick a random date. This date can be playful, sensual, erotic, orgasmic, etc... You don't necessarily know yet. However, the goal is to enjoy the journey and not the destination. There is no obligation to perform any act, this is an invitation to be truly yourself.

Both partners will take turns by asking the following 3 questions:

  1. - What do you truly desire?
  2. - What are you afraid of?
  3. - What do you love about me?
We recommend setting a timer of 5 Min per question and per person. The one who asks the question should not comment or say anything other the question that should be asked. Your role is to be fully present, listen, and ask the same question as many time as necessarily until the one answering the question says thank you. After your partner(s) says thank you, you can swap roles. Once everyone has listened and answered the first question, you can move on to the next question.

Press Play

Excited to start your date night ? We are too. With your partner(s), in your sacred space, you are going to hit that Play Now button. Read to your partner(s) the description of the date. Allow yourselves to be surprised and feel the excitement rising.

Sacred Ritual
15 Min - 30 Min

If you have already performed the sacred ritual, you can skip ahead. For returning players, we recommend that you read the date description and then perform with your partner(s) the sacred ritual. Remind each other of the love, devotion and passion that you have for each other. We recommend performing the ritual right after reading the description of the date night to your partner(s). The ritual is a great way to make yourselves fully present for one another.

If you get a date night that can not be performed, we recommend that you agree on a future occasion to enjoy this date night. You can pick another random date.

Enjoy your date night
1 Hour+

This is where you get crazy and wild. Enjoy your date night without holding back. During the date night, we recommend you and your partner(s) to always stay open for communications. Feel free to ask each other your boundaries.

Closing Ritual
15 Min

Each date night will suggest you a closing ritual that your partner(s) and you can perform. They will allow you to finish your evening in a beautiful way.
